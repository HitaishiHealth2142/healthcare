Comprehensive Eye Care

Welcome to Hitaishi Health Care Services – Eye Care Division. We provide cutting-edge diagnostics, compassionate care, and customized solutions for all your vision needs.

Our Eye Care Treatments

We diagnose and treat a wide range of vision problems with precision and care.

👁 Refractive Errors

Treatments for Myopia, Hyperopia, and Astigmatism including eyeglasses, contact lenses, and laser correction.

🧠 Cataract Surgery

Advanced cataract treatment and lens replacement surgeries with high precision and safety.

💉 Glaucoma Treatment

Early detection and pressure control through medication, laser therapy, or surgery.

🔬 Diabetic Retinopathy

Retinal care for diabetic patients using advanced imaging and laser treatment to prevent vision loss.

🔍 Regular Eye Checkups

We recommend periodic exams to maintain eye health, even without symptoms. Prevention is better than cure!

Total Vision Care
