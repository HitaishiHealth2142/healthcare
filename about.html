About Us

Founded in 2010, Hitaishi Healthcare is a NABH-accredited hospital dedicated to improving community health. Our 100-bed facility includes an advanced ICU, state-of-the-art operation theaters, and a team of over 50 experienced doctors and 200 dedicated staff members.

We believe in delivering accessible, affordable, and high-quality medical care to every patient. Our services span across multiple specialties including General Medicine, Pediatrics, Cardiology, Orthopedics, and Emergency Care.

Visit us to experience personalized care delivered by professionals who truly care. Your health is our priority.

Our Services

General Medicine

Comprehensive care for adults, including preventive check-ups and chronic disease management.

Pediatrics

Specialized care for children, from newborn check-ups to vaccinations and growth monitoring.

Diagnostics

Advanced diagnostic services, including lab tests, imaging, and health screenings.

Cardiology

Expert heart care, including ECG, stress tests, and non-invasive cardiac procedures.

Orthopedics

Treatment for bone and joint issues, including surgeries and rehabilitation programs.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency services with rapid response and critical care support.

Dermatology

Skin care solutions including acne treatment, allergy testing, and cosmetic dermatology.

ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)

Specialized ENT care including sinus treatment, hearing tests, and surgical interventions.
